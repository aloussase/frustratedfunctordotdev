About

Hi, I'm Alexander Goussas. I'm currently working as a software developer at Thoughtworks. Prior to this, I worked in national industry here in Ecuador at Tia S.A. and ESPOL. The last one is also the university where I studied Computer Science, but I abandoned my degree in the last semester for reasons. The project I was working on was a custom OTEL collector to attempt to reduce the performance impact of distributed tracing.

I was born here in Ecuador, but spent most of my childhood in Venezuela and Spain. Fun fact: I was in Spain when they won their first World Cup. In my free time, I love reading, learning (human) languages and working on side projects. My favourite literature genre is fantasy (I have Mistborn tattoos), but I also read philosophy and others.

This blog serves as a way to share the stuff I learn and that I think might be useful to others. If you have any comments or questions about any material on this site, feel free to send me an email.

This blog is made with SpringBoot and hosted on of my Raspberries and exposed to the world through CloudFlare tunnels because I don't trust myself to handle my own security.
